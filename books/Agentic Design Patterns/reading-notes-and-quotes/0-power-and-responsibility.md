# A Thought Leader's Perspective: Power and Responsibility

"The first time I experimented with one of the new agentic coding tools, I felt that familiar spark of magic. I tasked
it with a personal project I had never found the time for : migrating a charity website from a simple web builder to a
proper, modern CI/CD environment."

"This is where craft, culture, and a relentless focus on our principles become our essential guide."

We must:

- *Build with Purpose* - "ensuring that every agent we design starts form a clear understanding of the client problem
  whe are solving."
- *Looks around Corners* - "anticipating failure modes and designing systems that are resilient by design."
- *Inspire Trust* - "being transparent about our methods and accountable for our outcomes."

"Ultimately, this journey is not about replacing human ingenuity, but about augmenting it."

_Marco Argenti, CIO, Engineering, Goldman Sachs_
